Overview

Pace is a medical billing and patient rounding application designed for use by licensed healthcare providers and their affiliated practices. This Privacy Policy describes how Pace collects, uses, stores, and protects information entered into the application.

By using Pace, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Information We Collect

Pace collects only the information necessary to provide its billing and rounding functionality. This includes:

  • Provider account information (name, email address, practice ID)
  • Patient billing records (patient name, date of birth, date of service, diagnosis codes, procedure codes)
  • Photographs taken within the app for billing documentation purposes
  • Device information necessary for app functionality

Pace does not collect any information beyond what is required to operate the application.

Camera Access

Pace requests access to your device's camera solely to allow providers to photograph data for billing records. Photographs taken within the app are:

  • Stored securely in Google Firebase Storage
  • Accessible only to authenticated users within the same practice
  • Never shared with third parties or used for any purpose other than billing documentation
  • Deleted from storage when the associated patient record is removed

Camera access is used exclusively for billing documentation. Pace does not access your camera roll, location, microphone, or any other device sensors.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected by Pace is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Displaying and managing patient billing records within your practice
  • Generating billing reports and export documents (PDF and CSV)
  • Authenticating providers and maintaining practice-level access controls
  • Providing real-time data synchronization across devices within the same practice

Pace does not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the application.

Data Storage & Security

All data entered into Pace is stored using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Specifically:

  • Patient records are stored in Google Cloud Firestore
  • Facesheet photographs are stored in Google Firebase Storage
  • All data is scoped to your practice ID and accessible only to authenticated members of your practice
  • Authentication is managed through Google Firebase Authentication

While Pace implements strong technical security measures, no system is completely immune to security risks. Providers are responsible for maintaining the security of their login credentials.

Data Sharing

Pace does not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google Firebase — as our infrastructure provider, Google processes data in accordance with their terms of service and privacy policy
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Practice administrators — within your own practice, data is accessible to all authenticated providers sharing the same practice ID

Data Retention

Patient billing records are retained until explicitly deleted by an authorized provider within your practice. Photographs are deleted from Firebase Storage automatically when their associated patient record is permanently deleted.

Provider account data is retained for as long as the account remains active. Upon request, we will delete account data within a reasonable timeframe.

Healthcare Compliance

Pace is intended for use by licensed healthcare providers in a professional capacity. Providers using Pace are responsible for ensuring their use of the application complies with all applicable laws and regulations, including HIPAA, in their jurisdiction.

Practices requiring a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) should contact us before using Pace with protected health information (PHI).

Your Rights

As a user of Pace, you have the right to:

  • Access the data stored within your practice account
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your billing records at any time using the built-in export features

Children's Privacy

Pace is intended for use by licensed healthcare professionals only. The application is not directed at individuals under the age of 18 and we do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy
